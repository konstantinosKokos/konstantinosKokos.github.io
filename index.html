Hey!

I am Kokos. I am an engineer by trade; I received my diploma in electrical & computer engineering from the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki. After graduation, I spent a year working with the awesome kids of geekbot.io as a machine learning engineer while acquiring my master's degree in Artificial Intelligence from Utrecht University. For the last three years, I have been working under the supervision of prof. dr. Michael Moortgat in the NWO-funded project A composition calculus for vector-based semantic modelling with a localization for Dutch". My research revolves around type-driven methods for syntactic parsing and semantic compositionality. I am especially interested in the intersection of formal methods and machine learning; if that floats your boat, you can have a look at my publications page.

I am currently teaching the Logic & Language course in the AI Master's programme of Utrecht University. In previous years, I also helped as a TA for the following AI Master's courses:

  • Logic & Language (2018-2019, 2019-2020)
  • Machine Learning for Human Vision & Language (2019-2020)
  • Advanced Topics in Cognitive Modelling (2018-2019)
